About

I'm a UX/UI designer who designs and implements — product thinking, visual design, and frontend execution in the same role. Most of my work has been commercial digital products where design decisions get tested against real outcomes.

Currently I own Yogibo Singapore's entire digital operation — Shopify storefront, marketplace channels, frontend implementation, campaign visuals, performance, and CMS — as a one-person team.

Venezuelan-born, shaped by years in China and Singapore, now designing in Japan. I've worked across JP, SG, CN, IN, NZ, and UK team contexts. That range shapes how I read briefs, manage expectations, and design for users with different habits.
